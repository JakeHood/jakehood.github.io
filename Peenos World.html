Peenos World

Peenos world is a single level, 3rd person platformer made in UE4 and with the challenge of keeping it in a small space.

The player spawns in as peenos who's animations where made by me and they'll be told that they need to collect 4 gems to win.

The player can find mangos to collect for points and will be able to get a checkpoint for if they die to the monkeys.

The cutscene and map design was also done by me the sound effects as you walk around on different surfaces and even water also well as some spring pads.
