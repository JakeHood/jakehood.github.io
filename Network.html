A small 2v2 or 3v3 catpure and defence the flag game.

Start in a main menu where you can edit setting, host a game or join a game.

Once you're in a game you can pick your team, chatacter, run, jump around, type in a chat box and pick a username.

When the game starts the teams will be put to their spawn points and they will be able to pick up weapons, one player can also collect armour.

In the gameplay, the players can use knifes to have melee combat if a player dies they'll be moved to a spectator area and be able to watch the game from above. If all players on a team are dead or the attacking team get the flag back to their spawn the game will go into slowmo for 2 seconds and a point will be added to the score.

Then everyone will be teleported back to spawn for a second then the next round will restart and everyone will have full health and need to get weapons again.
