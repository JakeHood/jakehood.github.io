Jake Hood.

I am a Graduate Games Programmer from GloucesterShire University with experience in Unreal Engine 5 (Blueprints/C++) and Unity (C#) with 3 months of industry experience as an Intern working for World Makers on Deceit 2.

I am more confident when it comes to explaining it through speaking and showing how/why I've done things so I've made some videos to help get that across.

My industry experience comes from working on Deceit 2 as a programming intern but I did do some design things too, helping come up with and test new features as well as balance. I looked at adding roles to the game, looking at UI/UX for players to makes things clear while keeping the confusion a deception game needs.

I have lead a few group projects that ranged from 3 to 9 people that have gone very well. Starting with a 2D local VS game in Unity and in the most recently making a 3D 2 player local coop hoard shooter in UE5.

I am currently working on 2 projects with one being a 2 player social deduction in UE5 as a solo project but looking to team up with some people later in the project and in an indie company on a game while under NDA so detail will be given when I'm allowed to

I enjoy playing many types of games as well as playtesting new ones, the main type I play is competitive shooters like Marvel Rivals and Deadlock at the moment, while also really like social deduction games with friends. The video below goes into more detail about this.
