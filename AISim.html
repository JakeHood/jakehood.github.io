AI Simuation

Using UE5 a predator VS prey simulation was made using the Utility system and Behaviour trees.

Rabbits VS Foxes with stat bars to know what they need to keep themself alive. There is also a thought bubble above them so you can see what they're thinking and aiming to do

Rabbits can eat, drink, reproduct and hide

When Rabbits reproduct the gene pool can be effected in many ways, they can take can avgerage of the parents, they can take the best stats or a rare chance they mutate and have random stats
