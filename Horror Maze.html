Horror Maze

First person maze horror game made in 2 weeks for a games jam in UE4.

The player starts in the middle of the maze told to collect keys and then return back to the start for the lock to escape.

The AI will track and path find it's way to the player as the player gets more kills more spawn so they need to work their way around trying to not to be caught.

As they get to the last few keys the keys will become highlighted through walls to help them not endlessly be searching and then the lock will be highlight once all the keys have be found.
